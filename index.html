Our Principles:

Equality: We believe in the inherent worth and equality of all people. Our aid is provided impartially, ensuring that everyone, regardless of their background, receives the support they require.
Inclusivity: Our organization serves diverse communities around the world. We embrace diversity and strive to reach those who are most vulnerable, fostering a sense of belonging and solidarity.
Compassion: At Humanity First, we approach each person with empathy and understanding. We recognize the unique challenges faced by individuals and offer assistance with kindness and respect.

Humanity First, an organization established in the year 2015 is committed in making a positive impact on communities in need. Over the years, we have undertaken various projects aimed at improving the lives of individuals and families across the globe. From building toilets in remote villages to distributing food to those affected by natural disasters such as floods, our efforts have touched the lives of many.

We have more than 40 projects on education, healthcare, livelihood, and women empowerment in over 12,000 remote villages and urban slums across 47 counties of kenya humanitarian first works as a catalyst in the cycle of change, complementing and supplementing government efforts We sensitize and partner with like-minded institutions and individuals to implement high-impact programmes that enable access

Our humanitarian projects extend beyond immediate relief efforts. We strive to empower communities to become self-sufficient and resilient in the face of challenges. Through initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and economic empowerment, we aim to create lasting change and uplift those in need.

What We Do
Empowering Communities

At Humanity First, we believe in the power of community. Through our various programs and initiatives, we strive to empower individuals and communities to reach their full potential.

Join us in building a brighter future for all.
Healthcare for Humanity

Good health is essential for a fulfilling life. At Humanity First, we believe that access to healthcare is a fundamental human right. That's why we work tirelessly to provide medical assistance, healthcare services, and health education to communities in need.

Join us in our mission to ensure healthcare for all.
Education for All

Education is the cornerstone of progress and opportunity. At Humanity First, we are committed to ensuring that every child has access to quality education, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Together, we can make education a reality for all
Inspiring Change

Humanity First doesn't just respond to crises; we inspire change. Through our advocacy and outreach efforts, we raise awareness about the root causes of disasters and conflicts, driving systemic change to create a more just and compassionate world.

Lets inspire change.
Women and Girls Empowerment

Humanity First is committed to empowering women and girls worldwide, recognizing their crucial role in building resilient communities. Through education, economic opportunities, and advocacy for gender equality, we strive to break barriers, amplify voices, and create a future where every woman and girl can reach their full potential.

Join us in empowering our mothers and sisters.
Disaster Relief

In times of crisis, Humanity First responds swiftly to provide vital assistance to those affected by natural disasters, conflicts, and other emergencies. Our teams work tirelessly to deliver essential supplies, medical aid, and humanitarian support to help communities recover and rebuild.

Our humanity shines brightest through the relief we provide.
